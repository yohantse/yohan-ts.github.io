About EMWA

Building an Inclusive and Empowered Media Landscape

The Ethiopian Media Women Association (EMWA) is dedicated to promoting gender equality, professional development, leadership, and visibility for women media practitioners throughout Ethiopia.

Through advocacy initiatives, training programs, public engagement, partnerships, and research-driven work, EMWA strives to create a more inclusive and representative media environment.

EMWA envisions a future where Ethiopian women journalists and media professionals thrive as influential voices in journalism, storytelling, public dialogue, and leadership.
